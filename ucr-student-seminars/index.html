What is P&A Student Seminar?

P&A Student Seminar is a weekly research seminar organized by and for grad students in UCR Physics and Astronomy department. The primary purpose of this seminar is to create a safe place for grad students to present their research works, at the same time, practice how to ask/answer good questions. Thus, faculty members are discouraged to come to the seminar to ensure a less stressful environment for students.

Who can submit abstracts?

Everyone who is currently a UCR grad student can submit abstracts. External students from other schools please contact the organizers if you would like to give a talk.

We open for abstract submission starting from May 9, 2022. It's a weekly seminar. We will keep the seminar running until there's no presenter in the queue.
