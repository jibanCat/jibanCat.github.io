What are the P&A Student Seminar Series?

Due to many people in my cohort are graduating soon, we think we need a safe place to practice our research presentation skills, including giving talks and asking/answering questions. Having the skill to answer difficult questions promptly and appropriately is crucial during a job talk. Candidates are often judged by how they react to harsh questions. But often, the existence of faculty members in the seminar room is why students cannot practice their skills in asking/answering questions. We thus think it would be beneficial to have a seminar series organized for/by grad students. So we can have a safe place to ask stupid questions and give naive answers without mental stress.

We open for abstract submission starting from May 9, 2022. It's a weekly seminar. We will keep the seminar running until there's no presenter in the queue.
