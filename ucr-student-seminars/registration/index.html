While we target graduate students, postdocs or undergraduates are also welcome to participate or submit abstracts.
Please submit your abstract using the Google Form below if you want to give a presentation. If you just want to participate, there is no need to register. Just come to the seminar room at the time of the talk.

Any questions you have can be directed to Ming-Feng Ho at mho026@ucr.edu or other organizers.

We open for abstract submission starting from May 9, 2022. It's a weekly seminar. We will keep the seminar running until there's no presenter in the queue.
